Water Sports

This website is all about water sports. Wakeboarding, waterskiing, and tubing!

Below you will find three different types of water activities and information about them.

Wakeboarding

Personally, wakeboarding is one of my favorite summer water sports. I just learned how to do it last summer, but it is really easy to pick up. Wakeboarding is done on one snowboard-type board, with your feet facing perpendicular to the long side of the board.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t1AMcDlPW9o

Water Skiing

Water skiing is one of the most difficult water sports to learn, but it is a lot of fun once you get the hang of it. I have been waterskiing since I was around five, and it was the first water sport I ever learned. Knowing how to water ski makes it very easy to learn how to wakeboard, kneeboard, etc because it teaches you ballance on the water. Waterskiing is done on two long skis (just like snow skis) but as the skier's skill increases they may start slolom skiing, which is the sport of skiing on just one ski (pictured above).

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s0GWQMmRJE8

Tubing

Although tubing isn't a sport, it is still one of the most fun boating activities! Inner tubes can range from one to even five person seats, making them fun for everyone. Some might say that tubing doesn't take skill, but it definitely takes a lot of grip strength and bravery.
